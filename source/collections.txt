.. _collection-tab:

Collections Tab
---------------

The :guilabel:`Collections` tab lists the existing collections in a
database and some key statistics for these collections. From this tab,
you can also create or drop collections.

To access the :guilabel:`Collections` tab for a database, click on a
database on the left hand pane .
