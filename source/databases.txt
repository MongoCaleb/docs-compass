.. _database-tab:

Databases Tab
-------------

The :guilabel:`Databases` tab lists the existing databases for your
MongoDB deployment. From this tab, you can also create or delete
databases.

To access the :guilabel:`Databases` tab, click the connection home
|home| on the left hand pane.
